The big idea

Every modern architecture's backward pass decomposes into three structural rules (chain, fan-in, product) plus five closed-form Jacobian tricks (diagonal, sparse toeplitz, binary selection, rank-1 correction, outer product). The proof suite makes this claim concrete: every layer VJP is a HasVJP instance, every composition is vjp_comp, every residual is biPath_has_vjp, and the whole ViT backbone lands as a single HasVJPMat theorem derived from one axiom (mhsa_has_vjp_mat).

The resulting trust kernel: 30 axioms, zero sorrys. Hit the blueprint and click vit_body_has_vjp_mat to see the whole chain light up back to primitives.
